Stamina Module gives a boost to multiple stamina stats. Even for your mounts.

The module costs:
Learn:
 - Ren: 500
 - Exotic: 250
Craft:
 - Ren: 100
 - Exotic: 50

The module gives these:
 - Maximum Stamina: 20%
 - Stamina Regen: 10%
 - Stamina Consumed: -10%
 - Tool Stamina Action Cost: -10%
 - Tamed Creature Maximum Stamina: 30%
 - Tamed Creature Stamina Regen: 20%
